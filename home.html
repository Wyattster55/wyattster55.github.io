HP Compaq dc7800 Small Form Factor
By:Wyatt Ross & James Hildebrand

The HP Compaq was a series of computers by Hewlett-Packard mainly aimed at buisnesses and for educational purposes. The version we have is the dc7800 Small Form Factor. It has an Intel Core 2 Duo E6550 which runs at a speed of 2.33GHz, has 1 GB of RAM, an 80GB HDD, an integrated Intel GMA 3100 Dynamic Video Memory Technology, and runs Windows Vista Business
